`colored_stanford_bunny.ply`は、「Stanford University Computer Graphics Laboratory」が[The Stanford 3D Scanning Repository](https://graphics.stanford.edu/data/3Dscanrep/)で公開している「Stanford Bunny」モデルを一部着色したものです。

The `colored_stanford_bunny.ply` is a partially colored version of the "Stanford Bunny" model published by "Stanford University Computer Graphics Laboratory" in [The Stanford 3D Scanning Repository](https://graphics.stanford.edu/data/3Dscanrep/).
